Poochie Doo has been the corner of North Broadway’s go-to dog salon for years — the kind of place where the team knows your dog by name and your dog knows the way to the door. We’re a full-service grooming salon: wash, cut, nails, ears, and the finishing touches that send them home smelling great.

We work by appointment only so every dog gets real attention instead of being rushed through a line. Give us a call and we’ll find a time that works.

Bath & Brush
